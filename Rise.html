The Problem

TO ADDRESS THESE CHALLENGES, RISE OFFERS BEGINNER WORKOUT GUIDES, HELPING USERS FEEL MORE CONFIDENT AND SUPPORTED IN THEIR FITNESS JOURNEY.

The Research

The Goal
HOW can workout beginners at UMich make the gym experience more friendly and comfortable, and HOW can they easily find workout partners?
Preliminary Data 
Why Gym Stats
We discovered the reasons people go to the gym and analyzed their desires and motivations.
Society’s Priorities
People prioritize a sense of community and a positive experience above all else.
Competitive Analysis
Ultimately, we found that people go to the gym for two main reasons: 1) to find a sense of community 2) to have a positive interaction and experience.
However, the current Michigan gym environment and app do not adequately meet these needs
The Solutions
1. Match System
Each user has their own customizable profile, similar to other social media platforms, where they can personalize details like gender, bio, and username. Users can also input their workout schedule and preferences, such as their workout split.
The app allows users to swipe right on potential workout partners they like and left on those they don’t, helping them find someone to exercise with based on shared workout preferences.
2. Traffic System
The gym map window on the right side of the screen presents real-time traffic status, allowing users to view how busy the gym is and which equipment is occupied. Users can easily swap between different gyms to check their availability.
This feature operates similarly to Yelp, where users provide instant feedback to indicate which machines are currently in use, ensuring up-to-date information for a better gym experience.
3. Socialize + Build a Community
The app includes a messaging tab that allows users to communicate with their "matches" and schedule gym meetups through a built-in calendar function.
To enhance security, we have implemented verification systems similar to LinkedIn, ensuring that each user can only register one verified account, which helps prevent scammers. Additionally, users are encouraged to meet exclusively at the gym to ensure the safety of all participants.
4.Workout Guide
This feature will help workout beginners learn proper techniques, enabling them to improve their form and routines.
Users will benefit from a better environment, leading to higher quality workout sessions and overall fitness progress.

The Ideation

User Persona
Our group developed personas by reviewing interview data, which allowed us to gain insight into what UMich students desire for a better gym community through a deeper understanding of the users.
User Flow
We decided to create a gym partner app, modeled after dating apps, to help users find their ideal workout partners. After brainstorming, we developed a user flow to structure the mobile app, ensuring it effectively guides users toward achieving their three key goals.
  1. Gym Partner 
  2. Gym Availability 
  3. Gym Guides

The Design & Iteration

The Sketch
As the UX designer for this project, I started by sketching the gym app's core features—gym partner matching, gym availability, and gym guides. Inspired by dating apps like Tinder and Hinge, my goal was to create a seamless user experience that makes it easy to find workout partners and foster meaningful connections.
Sketching helped us visualize the app’s layout and refine the wireframes, ensuring that the final designs were intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing.

  2. Nav layout Issues: The overall navigation layout needs improvement.

  4. Drop Shadow: The shadow opacity is too strong and should be reduced.

The Reflection

User Testing 👩‍💻
Overall, the testing results were positive, with users especially appreciating the new gym traffic feature and the match system for finding workout partners.
After user testing, some users found the button design challenging, so I updated the icons and adjusted the navigation bar layout to enhance the overall user experience.
Lesson 💡
This project taught me the value of teamwork and communication. Each team member contributed equally, and through our collaborative discussions, I was able to design an app that aligned with our shared vision. Teamwork and communication enabled me to focus on the most critical needs in developing the app design solution. Additionally, the short-term nature of the project helped me improve my time management skills.
